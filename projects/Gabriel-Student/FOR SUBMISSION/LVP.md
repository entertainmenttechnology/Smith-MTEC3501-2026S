Least Viable Product (LVP) – Target for Culmination (Next Semester)

What will the user experience?

The user or, in my case, audience member will experience a presentation that talks about how emergent media technology can impact the theatrical industry and why we should embrace it.

What features must be complete for it to be “done”?

There needs to be a minimum of two working demos, a run of show, and a TouchDesigner cue system. 

What technologies or platforms are involved?

TouchDesigner, Projection Mapping, Body Tracking, Image Replacement, Live Interactive Media, etc.

How will you measure success?

Success will largely be measured based on how effective my presentation is. Does it cover what it needs to cover? Is it interesting to watch? Does the audience feel engaged? Are people learning?
