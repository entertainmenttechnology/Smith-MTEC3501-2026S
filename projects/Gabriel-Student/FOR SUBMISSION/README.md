Gabriel Aguilar’s “Utilizing Emerging Technology Within Theatrical Settings” is a TED-style presentation exploring the evolving relationship between theater and emergent media technologies. Grounded in the concept of Gesamtkunstwerk, the “total work of art” first theorized by Richard Wagner, the project argues that contemporary theater must evolve to fully absorb computational and interactive technologies as artistic disciplines in their own right. Through historical analysis, technical breakdowns, and live demonstrations utilizing tools such as TouchDesigner, livestreaming systems, projection mapping, computer vision, and virtual production workflows, the presentation investigates how theatrical spaces can become reactive, immersive, and networked environments. The project ultimately proposes a future in which theater operates not simply as a staged performance but as a unified computational system integrating architecture, media, performance, and audience interaction into one holistic artistic organism.
