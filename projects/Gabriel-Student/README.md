Running Man is a tech-integrated multi-media performance that explores the physical and emotional weight of expectation through an interactive system. I chose this project to reflect on the personal tension many feel between the responsibilities to their loved ones and their own growth. To convey the emotional notes of the piece, I intend to use a treadmill and a live website to turn "self-martyrdom" into a frantic, unsettling experience.
